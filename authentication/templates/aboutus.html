Our Story

EDAS Consulting Services, founded in 2018 by Silas Magawi and Pauline Rogito, was born out of a shared vision to revolutionize HR solutions for organizations and individuals. Guided by a profound belief in the inherent value of people, our company is dedicated to transforming the human resource experience into an exhilarating journey. From the very beginning, EDAS Consulting Services set out to empower young individuals through personal development initiatives. Recognizing the transformative power of investing in the potential of the next generation, we committed ourselves to unlocking their talents and nurturing their growth. As time went on, our dedication and passion resonated with the community, leading to our rapid expansion. Responding to the diverse needs of our clients, we broadened our horizons and embraced the entire spectrum of human resources. Today, we stand as a comprehensive consultancy, providing specialized training, strategic advisory services, and holistic HR solutions.

Operating primarily in Kenya, our home country, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner in the field. With a team of over 100 talented professionals, we have successfully supported numerous businesses and individuals in their HR endeavors. We take immense pride in our achievements and the positive impact we have made on the lives of those we serve. Looking ahead, our aspirations extend far beyond our current accomplishments. EDAS Consulting Services aims to continue growing, expanding our reach, and making a difference in the lives of even more organizations and individuals. We are committed to remaining at the forefront of innovation in HR, consistently delivering simplified solutions that drive success, fulfillment, and growth. Join us on this exciting journey as we uplift the value in people, transform HR experiences, and contribute to a thriving future for all. Together, we can unlock the full potential of individuals, businesses, and communities, creating a world where every resource is harnessed to its greatest advantage.

We are here for one reason:

To be the HR consultancy firm of choice to individuals and organizations for world-class human resource management solutions.

And Why:

Our capabilities are completely scalable and our processes provide industry-leading results, regardless of the size of your company or project. We are committed to serving your human resource needs both at an individual and corporate level. In serving our clients, we take a keen interest in the unique nature of their business needs and work closely with them in order to meet the specific needs. We go an extra mile in our delivery and leave nothing to chance. We aim to build trust among our clients in a bid to ensure that they can rely on us for any HR need.

Our Approach

In undertaking the assignments, we focus on but are not limited to employment laws, organization goals, vision and strategies, best labour practices, clients’ specific needs etc. We believe an interactive approach, plus our consultants’ expertise and state-of-the-art methodologies, are directly responsible for our extraordinary success rate. Our approach, built on immersive collaboration, customer-focused design thinking, and comprehensive solutions, enables EDAS Consulting to provide business and HR leaders with the necessary momentum to predict and react to disruptive forces. As a firm, we use the consultative process approach whereby the stakeholders will be consulted at every stage where necessary. This will encourage positive participation and ownership of the assignment. It is crucial to have senior management support, especially where policies relate to employee behaviour. The endorsement and modelling of the behaviour by the CEO, senior managers and supervisors will encourage staff to take the policies and changes seriously.
